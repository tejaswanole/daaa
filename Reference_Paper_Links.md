## Tips for Downloading

1. **Open Access papers** ([1], [5], [6], [8]): Click the DOI link → download PDF directly
2. **Paywall papers**: Use your **VIIT/VIT institutional login** via the library portal, or check if the paper is available on **ResearchGate** (many authors upload preprints)
3. **Google Scholar alternative**: Go to https://scholar.google.com and paste the paper title — often shows free PDF links on the right side
4. **Sci-Hub**: For any DOI-based paper, go to `sci-hub.se` and paste the DOI
